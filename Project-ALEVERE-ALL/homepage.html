Welcome to the

Alevere Clinics

For Aesthetic and Preventive Medicine

We offer a full range of the latest and most advanced treatments in cosmetic medicine and non-surgical weight loss, for which our expertise has become widely known.

Dr Mark Palmer, our medical director, is a longstanding member of the British Association of Cosmetic Doctors and is a recognised expert in the field of non-surgical facial rejuvenation.

All of our staff carrying out cosmetic treatments have been personally trained by Dr Palmer to the highest standards. Please see our treatment menu on the left for current information about all the non-surgical cosmetic treatments which we have available.
