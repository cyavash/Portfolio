Let's go to New York City!

The city of fashion, culture, and culinary experiences. In New York City you can have anything you want - with the exception of sleep.

In A Nutshell

New York City is the perfect destination to visit for a weekend, a week, the summer, or maybe you'll end up moving here? There is always something to do and something for everyone. It is made up of five boroughs - Manhattan, Brooklyn, Queens, the Bronx, and Staten Island.

For the first time visitors, you have to see the touristy things- you just have to. However, definitely make time to do as the locals do! We'll provide you with the must see tourist spots that you see in every movie but also the cool places that New Yorkers frequent.
